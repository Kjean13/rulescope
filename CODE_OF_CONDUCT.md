# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Behavior that contributes to a positive environment includes:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
- Derogatory or insulting comments, personal or political attacks
- Publishing private information without permission

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

## Enforcement

Report issues to jeank@eliesec.fr. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly.
